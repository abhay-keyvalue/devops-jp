Prologue

In an alternate reality, the events of Avengers: Infinity War take a harrowing turn as Tony Stark, the brilliant inventor known as Iron Man, fails to create a time machine to undo the devastation caused by the dreaded Thanos. Instead, in the aftermath of the catastrophic snap that obliterates half of all living creatures, the mighty Titan attempts to destroy the Infinity Stones, the source of his terrifying power. Yet, as the Infinity Gauntlet unleashes its might, an unforeseen surge of energy disperses the Stones across the vast expanse of the universe, rendering them virtually impossible to retrieve. In the wake of this unimaginable tragedy, the Avengers find themselves facing their greatest challenge yet. With half of all life wiped out and the Stones scattered far and wide, the universe plunges into chaos and despair. In this grim reality, only four Avengers remain to bear the weight of the world's salvation - Captain America, the steadfast leader; Iron Man, the genius and heart of the team; Thor, the mighty god of thunder; and Hulk, the indomitable force of nature. Unwilling to accept defeat, the remaining Avengers are driven by an unwavering determination to reunite the Stones and harness their combined power to reverse the devastation caused by Thanos' snap. Their mission is no longer simply to defeat a formidable enemy; it is now a race against time to bring back the half of the living race that was lost. As they embark on their perilous journey across the cosmos, the Avengers must confront their inner demons, face new and treacherous foes, and forge alliances with unlikely allies. Sacrifices will be made, and the bonds of friendship and camaraderie will be put to the ultimate test. Amidst the darkness that envelops the universe, they hold on to the hope that the Infinity Stones can be retrieved and used to mend the shattered universe.

Assemble your avengers, for you hold the key to reassembling the scattered Infinity Stones and restoring balance to a fractured cosmos. But time is of the essence, and the clock is ticking. Will you be the heroes that the universe needs?
